Matt Ramage

During this episode of The Green Bay Narrative, I sit down with the creator of Packers Memes, now known better as Matt Ramage. During this conversation, which was recorded prior to his page name change, we discuss what made him decide to start his page, what it was like growing up in Green Bay, the time he hung out with Tom Crabtee, and much more. Matt and I go way back, so it was great to sit down and get to hear his stories. I hope you enjoy hearing it as much as I enjoyed listening to it. You can find Matt on Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram.
