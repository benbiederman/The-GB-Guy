Zak & Ashli

On this episode of The Green Bay Narrative, we mix it up a little bit. I sit down with not one Packers fan, but two big Packers fans. Zak and Ashli reside near the heart of enemy territory in Minnesota. Despite living in Minnesota, they proudly wear Packers gear and surround themselves with like-minded individuals. These two cheeseheads also made a trip to an iconic but notorious stadium this past season. During this conversation Zak and Ashli share with me what made them Packers fans while living in Minnesota, their trip to Soldier Field, what it’s like to live in Minnesota during football season, and much more.
