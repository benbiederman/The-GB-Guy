Kavarna Coffeehouse

Located on North Broadway in downtown Green Bay, is Kavarna Coffeehouse. Kavarna Coffeehouse has been serving the Green Bay community delicious coffee, baked goods, and food since 1999. Let’s explore how Kavarna Coffeehouse is stacking up compared to some of the other coffeehouses in Green Bay.

What to expect

When going to Kavarna Coffeehouse the first thing to call out is that the parking isn’t as plentiful as some of the other coffeehouses in Green Bay. Depending on when you go to Kavarna Coffeehouse you may need to pay for parking since it’s downtown, otherwise you’ll have to walk a bit more to get to Kavarna Coffeehouse.

Kavarna Coffeehouse is a deceivingly large space. Upon entering the building, it seems like a small coffee shop, however, once you get around the counter you see that the building goes quite far back. Kavarna Coffeehouse offers a lot of seating for their patrons. It makes Kavarna Coffeehouse a great place to get some coffee or something to eat while doing a bit of work.

One thing I will call out, while they do offer breakfast and lunch options on their menu, I will say their food menus are very limited. If you’re thinking of getting food from Kavarna Coffeehouse, I’d encourage that you check their menu first to make sure they offer something you’ll want.

Overall, Kavarna Coffeehouse offers a great atmosphere for anyone looking to get something to eat or drink. If you’re coming to Green Bay and looking for a great cup of coffee, Kavarna Coffeehouse could be the place you’re looking for.
