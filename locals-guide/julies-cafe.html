Julie's Cafe

With multiple locations throughout the Green Bay area, Julie’s Cafe is a popular location, known in the area for their great breakfast. Their large portion sizes of great food at a great price takes the guess work out of why it’s so popular in the area. Julie's Cafe offers a nice breakfast menu as well as lunch and dinner options.

What to expect

As stated above, you get a whole lot of food at a great price. My two blueberry pancakes (pictured above), were less than $5. Their prices don’t take away from quality one bit, their food doesn’t seem to cut corners when it comes to quality. The hashbrowns are made in house, not poured out of a bag purchased from a distributor. With food this good, there’s no wonder why there are multiple locations through-out Green Bay.

All in all, pretty straight forward review. If you're looking for a great place to grab a nice, affordable breakfast, look no further than Julie's Cafe.
