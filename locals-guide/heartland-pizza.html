Heartland Pizza Co.

As an individual who has a body that is built by pizza, anytime I have the opportunity to try a new pizza place, I’m excited to dive in. Heartland Pizza Co. opened in 2017 and hasn't looked back since. Heartland Pizza Co. offers a nice, unique menu which will have something for anyone.

What to expect

The location Heartland is in, is somewhat tucked behind Oneida Street. If you’re not from the area, you may not see it from a main street, but if you utilize GPS, you should be able to find it no problem. One nice part, if you're coming to Green Bay for a Packers game, it's pretty close to Lambeau. You won't have to journey far to get some damn good pizza.

What I really liked about Heartland Pizza is the major focus on natural products. Darin, the owner, walked us through the menu and explained their concept. One thing that I really like about Heartland is their emphasis on real food. What I mean by that is they focus on handcrafting their food. Dough is made in house daily, fresh veggies, premium meats and cheeses. They don't even offer soft drinks like Coca-Cola or Pepsi due to the corn syrups used, instead they have premium sodas that use cane sugar. It was great quality without a huge hit to the wallet.

Since my initial visit to Heartland I've rewrote this review a couple of times and even increased the score. Every time I go to Heartland Pizza Co. I question why I don't go there more often. The freshness, great staff, and amazing product makes Heartland Pizza Co. a winner every time.

Darin and his team are continously adding delicious items to their menu. Recently they decided to start offering fresh pasta that is made in house. If you're looking for a great place to eat, that is also close to Lambeau Field, this is the spot for you. Go visit Darin and his team at Heartland Pizza Co and get yourself a great meal.
