Sara's Artisan Gelato

Located just off of Oneida Street, which means it's close to Lambeau Field, is Sara's Artisan Gelato. Sara's Artisan Gelato, if the name didn't give it away, is known for their artisan gelato. Along with gelato, they also serve up some delicious sorbet.

What to expect

The first thing I noticed when I walked into Sara's Artisan Gelato was how clean it felt. They did a fantastic job of creating a space that feels very clean, yet inviting. They have a nice wall with decor that allows you take a photo of your gelato for the gram, if you're into that sort of thing.

Sara's Artisan Gelato makes their gelato with the highest quality ingredients. They offer a wide variety of gelato and sorbet options, you'll be able to find something for anyone. During my visit to Sara's Artisan Gelato, I tried the Halloween candy flavor of gelato. I elected to have it over a warm brownie, which was a nice upgrade option. The portion size is deceiving; the gelato mixed with the brownie was so rich that it was hard to even finish the dessert. I mean... I still did because it was that good, but it was a tough finish.

One final thing to call you, you can also find Sara's Artisan Gelato at Angelina and in the Titletown District.
