Copper State Brewing Co.

Beer, coffee, and great food, what more could you possibly want? Copper State Brewing Co. is a relatively new joint in Green Bay, taking over the space that Hinterland used to occupy. Was I hopped up after how great my Copper State Brewing Co. experience was, or did the experience come out flat?

What to expect

BEER. COFFEE. GREAT FOOD. What more do you need to know?! That's the diet of most Wisconsinites. Copper State Brewing Co. is supplying the food and beer, while Copper Rock Coffee (located inside of Copper State Brewing Co.) is supplying the delicious coffee. One thing I really like about Copper State Brewing Co. is the fact that they utilize a lot of ingredients from local companies in their dishes, for example the bacon they use is from Nueske's Smoked Meats.

We had to try the loaded beer fries, fries loaded with a beer cheese, bacon, and other good stuff. It was a must have for our trip. The ratio of cheese sauce and bacon to the fries was just right. Really set a nice tone for the rest of the meal.

Copper State Brewing Co is really doing the right things. The vibe in their restaurant is outstanding. It's a place I look forward to dining at or grabbing a coffee from in the future.

Rating

4.4
