Cheese Cake Heaven

Located near Lambeau Field on Oneida Street is Cheese Cake Heaven. Known for their delicious desserts and food, Cheese Cake Heaven offers over 90 different dessert items, baked fresh in house daily. While they offer many different dessert options, Cheese Cake Heaven is known for their cheesecake.

What to expect

Cheese Cake Heaven is quite a large space. Don’t be discouraged if their parking lot is full, as the size of the store far exceeds their parking lot. Before heading to Cheese Cake Heaven, I’d highly recommend browsing their menu online to get an idea of what you want before heading to Cheese Cake Heaven. They don’t necessarily have a menu board at the location, so if you’re unsure of what you want, they offer paper menus at the register to browse while determining what you’re looking to eat.

Cheese Cake Heaven offers a variety of delicious food items, made with fresh ingredients. When ordering food from Cheese Cake Heaven, you can expect to get a nice, fresh meal. The turnaround time for your food is usually on the slower side, which is by no means a deal breaker, but something to expecting when you go to Cheese Cake Heaven.

Finally, if you’re looking to go to Cheese Cake Heaven just for some delicious desserts, Cheese Cake Heaven offers their deserts to go. You can purchase whole cheesecakes or by the slice.
