The Bar

Not just any bar, but The Bar. Located within walking distance of Lambeau Field, The Bar offers a nice variety of menu options. Combine this with the fact that it’s a bar as well (I’m sure you could have never guessed that from the name alone), The Bar is a good place to grab some food/drinks before or after a game. Hell, it’s even a good place to watch the game if you’re in the area.

What to expect

The Bar has multiple locations in Green Bay and throughout Wisconsin. The owners and operators of The Bar are also the owners and operators of Hagemeister Park and Graystone Ale House. While they may own those other businesses, The Bar is my favorite of the three.

Honestly, the thing I really enjoy about The Bar is their menu. They offer a really nice variety of menu items. My go-to items when I go there, though, are wings or pizza. The Bar offers a wide array of homemade sauces to complement their wings. My only complaint on their wings is that they’re on the smaller side, but given the price of the wings, it doesn’t seem like you’re being ripped off. Get yourself some fries or cheesecurds to go with the wings and you’ll be golden. The pizza was a nice surprise. I wasn’t sure what to expect since bar pizzas can be hit or miss, but The Bar served up some tasty pizza pies the few times I’ve gotten them. The only caveat, the pizzas are a little on the pricier side.

Overall, if you're looking for a place to grab some food close by Lambeau Field, The Bar may be just what you're looking for.
