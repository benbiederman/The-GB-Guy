The Abbey

Located next to St. Norbert College, and known as somewhat of a college bar, is The Abbey. The Abbey offers a unique atmosphere with a vast menu of food and drinks. I had heard some good things about The Abbey, I was excited to give it a try.

What to expect

The first thing I noticed when I walked into the Abbey was the unique environment. The walls are lined with items from throughout the years. They have a lot of vintage items - both local and national memorabilia. I had to snap a photo of it because of awesome I thought it was.

As far as the food, it was great quality. My only gripe was the waitress didn't disclose there were onions in the wrap when I asked what was all on it. After getting it corrected, I was pretty pleased with the wrap. The crunchy chicken mixed with the buffalo and ranch, it added a nice little zip from the buffalo and good flavor with the ranch.

One thing that was quite unique to me that I'd like to call out, when paying the bill, there are two different tip lines. There's a tip line for the waitstaff and one for the kitchen staff. I definitely wasn't prepared for that and wasn't sure what breakdown I should do for it. I've never seen anything like it before. I wanted to add something about it here so you can plan your attack a bit better than I did.

All in all, The Abbey was a place I'd go again to eat. It wouldn't jump off a list of places to see in Green Bay for me, but it was pretty solid and left me with a good impression for the most part.

Rating

4.0
