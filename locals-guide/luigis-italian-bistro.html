Luigi's Italian Bistro

Luigi’s Italian Bistro is located hidden away in Eastern Green Bay. Known for their delicious pizza and Italian food, Luigi’s Italian Bistro has been serving delicious food to the greater Green Bay area for years.

What to expect

The first thing to call out about Luigi’s Italian Bistro is that I think they’re somehow related to Luigi’s Pizza Palace II. The menu is similar and the food is identical. In fact, until doing my Local’s Guide content, I thought this location was Luigi’s Pizza Palace, making sense as to why the other one was Luigi’s Pizza Palace II.

Beyond being identical to Luigi’s Pizza Palace II, Luigi’s Italian Bistro offers some great food at a damn good price. I’m always surprised by the amount of food you get for your money and never find myself ordering an appropriate amount of food due to their portion quantities.

Go do yourself a favor, get some beer nuggets and a pizza from either Luigi's Italian Bistro or Luigi's Pizza Palace II, you won't be disappointed.

4.4
