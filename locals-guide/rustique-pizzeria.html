Rustique Pizzeria

If you're like me, you love yourself some pizza. It seems like there are so many pizza places in every town now, they're a dime a dozen. Rustique Pizzeria breaks away from your normal pizza joint. For starters, their restaurant is located in an old church. Rustique Pizzeria has a one of a kind feel thanks to this feature. I honestly don't know how to describe Rustique Pizzeria. It has just a beautiful vibe. In the summer you can sit out on their deck and enjoy some pizza along with good company. Seriously, just writing this makes me want to head to Rustique for a pizza.

What to expect

Odds are it'll be busy, it's usually a popular spot. Trust me, it's worth it to stick it out and have a drink or two at the bar while you wait for your spot. Their pizza is made in a brick fire oven, which results in a nice crispy crust that's still chewy. Also, expect to see some interesting combinations of pizzas. They're always coming up with different specialty pizzas to roll out. Fear not though, they still offer the classic pizzas you'd look to find at other joints.

If you take anything away from this review, other than going to Rustique, let it be this - their pizza is outstanding but to make your Rustique Pizzeria experience complete you need to get their pestro fries. Their pesto fries are one of the best things I've ever had. A crunchy bed of fries, topped with the perfect balance of pesto, balsamic glaze, and parmesan cheese. I'm craving them just typing this up.

Rustique Pizzeria is a must-go to spot when in the Green Bay area. You won't be disappointed in your Rustique Pizzeria experience. Oh, and don't forget about the pesto fries.
