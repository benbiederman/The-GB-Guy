Sammy's Pizza

Located less than a mile from Lambeau Field is Sammy's Pizza, a family owned and operated Italian restaurant. Sammy's Pizza is a favorite amongst those in the community. They pride themselves on homemade, high quality dishes with fresh ingredients. Red Batty, Packers equipment manager, always tells people who are waiting outside of Lambeau for autographs about Sammy's.

What to expect

The first thing I noticed was when going to Sammy's you'll have a slightly more expensive pie. A small pepperoni, which is pictured above, is a 10'' thin crust pizza and it cost me $14. However, given the quality of the food, a higher price is expected. It's a family-owned and family-operated business that has been creating delicious pizza and paste since the 40s. Per their website, their receipes haven't changed since founding the restaurant; which is evident by how good of quality of a pizza you get.

Fred (@beanfm) on Twitter also mentioned that they have a game day special at Sammy's. You pay $20 to park at Sammy's during a Packers game and in return get a $20 gift card to the restaurant. That is a deal you can't beat if you're planning on going to the game.

Aside from the price, which wasn't TOO terrible, the pizza was great quality and I would gladly eat another pie from Sammy's.
