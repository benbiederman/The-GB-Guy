China Kitchen

China Kitchen is one of those restaurants that is a hidden gem of Green Bay. Located on the East side of Green Bay, you really have to go off the beaten path as a tourist to find China Kitchen. Known for their great food at a great value, China Kitchen is a place that has a lot to offer.

What to expect

The first thing that always catches my eye when I go to China Kitchen is a sign that states, “Our food has the lowest price, the highest value, the best taste, larger portions. Please give it a try.” At first it seems like an odd attempt, almost desperate, to get more customers. However, despite some slight puffery, I’d agree with this sign.

I’ve been to China Kitchen numerous times and the experience has always been great. My girlfriend and I usually get a normal portion of our dish and an egg roll, the total always seems to be around $12 - $13. The food itself is always fresh, hot, and plentiful.

Honestly, if I’m craving Chinese food, China Kitchen is usually my spot. If you're looking to find a Chinese food joint that has the lowest price, highest value, best taste, and larger portions, please give it a try.
