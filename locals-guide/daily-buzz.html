Daily Buzz Espresso Bar

The Daily Buzz Espresso Bar is located in a beautiful corner business of a building in downtown Green Bay. Known for their drinks involving espresso, Daily Buzz Espresso Bar isn't just limited to espresso beverages. They also serve coffees, teas, and smoothies along with their delicious espresso drinks to make sure they offer something for any person.

What to expect

The location Daily Buzz Espresso Bar is located in, is an absolutely beautiful location with a lot of natural light. It’s a very cool location if you were wanting to grab a coffee and do a little bit of work, or just relax and enjoy the atmosphere. The only draw to the location is that parking can tend to be limited where it is located, so you may have to walk a bit more to get to Daily Buzz Espresso Bar. Also, if you go to Daily Buzz Espresso Bar on a Saturday in the Summer months, you’ll have to navigate the Farmer’s Market to get to it. It’s not a huge deal if you know about it up front, but something to call out.

Similar to Luna Coffee Roasters, if you’re in a big rush you may want to go elsewhere to grab a cup of deliciousness. They don’t offer a drive-thru for their guests, so it can be more of a timely process to get in and get your drink. I wouldn’t let that discourage you from trying Daily Buzz Espresso Bar, while it can be a bit of a wait, I’ve never had an experience where it’s an outrageous wait time. I will also tell you that the wait has always been worth it in my experiences

My biggest complaint with Daily Buzz Espresso Bar is that they can run of certain ingredients for their popular drinks quite often. An example of this is they tend to always be out of the horchata for their horchata latte. While it’s not a huge deal since they serve up a lot of different, delicious drinks, just something that can make it annoying when you have your heart set on one drink in particular.

All in all, Daily Buzz Espresso bar serves up some absolutely delicious drinks. They offer a variety of seasonal beverages too to keep their menu fresh and interesting. I’d definitely encourage anyone to give them a try if they’re looking for a great place to grab a cup of their daily buzz.
