Happy Joe's

Much like Joe, anytime I get to try a new pizza place I'm happy. The new pizza place this round is Happy Joe's located on the East side of Green Bay. Happy Joe's is known for their wide variety of crust choices and Mexican inspired pizzas. For the taste-test round we got a traditional pepperoni pizza.

What to expect

The first thing I noticed about Happy Joe's when I took a bite was their tomato sauce is different than a lot of other traditional sauces. To me and my palate, that's as sophisticated as a 7-year-old's palate, it didn't really compliment the pizza. It felt like it just didn't fit with the pizza we ordered. I'm not sure if it's a sauce that compliments their Mexican inspired pizzas more or what exactly, but it just didn't flow in my opinion.

Happy Joe's wasn't a bad pizza, it was just different than what I prefer with the sauce style they use. If given the choice between going to Happy Joe's or another pizza joint like Heartland or Rustique, I'd pick either Heartland Pizza or Rustique Pizzeria instead. I'd gladly try Happy Joe's again, it just wouldn't be my go-to pizza place.

Rating

3.75
